Dublin's first Dog only Sports & Leisure centre

POOL

Check out our pool area!
Our exclusive 10m swimming pools are fitted
for every breed. Our pools are equiped with
16kW Hewalex heat pumps to ensure pleasant
temperatures all year round, cleaned thoroughly
throughout the day with a brand new filtering
system to make sure yours and your dogs health
are a top priority. We offer free swimming
lessons to pups under 12 months. All our
swimming pools are PETA approved.

PLAYGROUND

Visit our playground!
Formerly known as St. Anne's park, newly
renovated, fully equiped dog playground.
Ranging from professional training equipment
to chewy toys. We have it all!
The playground is spread acros 100 acres to
accommodate all your needs. Our playground is
fully fenced and monitored through the day
offering a safe environment for you and your
pup!

GYM

Try out our gym!
Ireland's first dog only gym! Need help
preparing for a competition or just staying in
shape, we got you covered! With more than 250
machines available, all ground floor for
accessibility, free lockers and staff on site ready
to help! The gym is thoroughly cleaned and
sanitezed after every visit.
